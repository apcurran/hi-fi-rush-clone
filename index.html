Hi-Fi RUSH game

An all-new rhythm-action game form Tango Gameworks

Labeled “defective” after an experiment accidentally fuses a music player into his heart, wannabe rockstar Chai must break free from a sinister corporation with raucous rhythm-enhanced combat - from the studio that brought you The Evil Within and Ghostwire: Tokyo!

Feel the beat and fight back

The whole world of Hi-Fi Rush moves to the beat, from Chai himself to the environment and even enemy attacks. Explore and fight with slick animated visuals that automatically match up to the beat. It doesn't just look cool - brawling to the beat amps up your combos too!

Heroes

Chai

A slacker with larger-than-life ambitions, Chai's life is turned upside down when a botched corporate experiment accidently gives him musical robot powers.

Labeled a defect, Chai will have to rely on his pals, including his mechanical cat assistant 808, to fight back against the megacorp looking to "recall" him.

Peppermint

Highly talented in robotics, Peppermint is the mind behind 808, an adorable cat-drone that provides Chai support both in and out of battle.

Peppermint herself is equally helpful, able to tag in to assist Chai with a volley of laser blasts to juggle enemies, overload barriers or even hit out-of-reach switches in the environment.

Macaron

Disillusioned by the megacorp's latest tactics, Macaron joins Chai's cause to bring the organization down before it's too late!

With a heart as big as his mighty arms, Macaron abhors violence but does everything he can to protect his friends. With the right encouragement, Macaron will help you break down obstacles - both those in Chai's path and the actual armor on enemies!

CNMN

CNMN (pronounced "cinnamon") is Macaron's robotic psychological analyst and offers their assistance to Chai and the gang.

Extremely chipper but also very blunt, CNMN's personality can take a little getting used to. Thankfully, CNMN likes to draw expressions on their face to get the point across.

Take down an evil megacorp, one boss at a time

Lead a squad of colorful allies and take the fight to the heart of an, er, heartless organization! From Production to Marketing, every department has it out for the “defective” Chai and will protect the bottom line in over-the-top battle sequences - with each showdown featuring its own music tracks!
